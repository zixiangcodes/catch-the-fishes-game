Instructions

- This game is mainly for kids to play.

- To win this game, get the highest score!

* Click on any fish with an open mouth or a starfish.
* You have 40 seconds for each time you start the game.
* The fishes will change every 4 seconds. So, you must click as many fishes within those 4 seconds!
* Each 🐠 fish with an open mouth you click, gives you 1 point! 😜
* Each 🐟 fish with a closed mouth you click, gives no point! 😓
* The ⭐ starfish gives you 3 points! 🤩
* The 🐙 jellyfish takes away 3 points! 😭
* You get 🎶 special winning jingles when you get either 1-9, 10-19, 20-29 or more than 30 points!
